Weiwei Sun

I'm an internship at Baidu Search in Beijing. Before that, I received my B.E. and M.E. from Shandong University in 2021 and 2023, respectively, where I was advised by Zhaochun Ren. I am interested in natural language processing and information retrieval, with an emphasis on large language models (LLMs) for information access, generative retrieval, and augmented language models..
